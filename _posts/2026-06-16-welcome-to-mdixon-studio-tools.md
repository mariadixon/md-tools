## Let Your Imagination Drive the Design

Welcome to the very first post on MDixon Studio Tools! 

If you have ever opened an industry-standard design program and felt like you were staring at an airplane cockpit, you are exactly who this site is for. 

Over my years of teaching Digital Media and Marketing, I've watched incredibly creative people retreat back to limiting, drag-and-drop apps simply because professional software was too intimidating. My goal here is to change that. 

I'll be using this space to share accessible frameworks, stripped-down workspaces, and custom scripts designed to take the friction out of professional design. These tools are built to flatten the learning curve so you can skip the software struggle and get straight to the part that actually matters: surprising yourself with what you are capable of creating.

Stay tuned—the first batch of tools is on the way!
